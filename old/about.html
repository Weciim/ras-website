About Us

Our missioon

Is to inspire the innovator in everyone and build a greater world, a world of curiosity and creativity. We help our member to develop their knowledge and practical skills in this fields through workshops, projects and competitions.

Our target

Is to be the most inspiring, challenging and highly-qualified community as well as a compassionate family.
We are passionate about our work and about working together. We commit to becoming a place where we do what’s perfect because we love what’s perfect.
Thus, if you are interested by the world of robotics, obsessed with new technologies and innovations feel free to join us.
